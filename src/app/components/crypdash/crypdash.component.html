The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your cryptocurrency portfolio. It's designed to give you quick insights into your investments with all the essential information at a glance.

Easily add new cryptocurrencies to your portfolio by filling out the form with details such as amount, purchase price, and total in $USD.
Modify existing cryptocurrency details with the update form. Simply adjust the necessary fields to keep your portfolio up to date.

This table provides a detailed list of all the cryptocurrencies you hold with solting functionality, including the name of each cryptocurrency, the total profit or loss for each, the quantity you own, and the the current market price of your holdings.

The crypto cards display key information for each cryptocurrency in your portfolio, including the name and balance, amount owned, total profit in both percentage and dollar value, purchase price, and today's price. Each card also features edit and delete actions for easy management of your holdings.

This pop-up appears to confirm your action to delete a cryptocurrency from your portfolio, ensuring you don't accidentally remove any holdings.

After successfully adding a new cryptocurrency to your portfolio, a confirmation message appears to let you know the addition was successful.

A detailed description of a selected cryptocurrency, including the name, price change (1 hour, 1 day, 1 week), current price ($USD, BTC) and market cap, offering in-depth insights.

This chart visually represents the distribution of your portfolio balance across different cryptocurrencies, helping you understand your investment allocation at a glance.
