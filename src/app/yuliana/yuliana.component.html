Yuliana Dementyeva Portfolio

Welcome to the web portfolio of Ukrainian top model Yuliana Dementyeva! This eye-catching and user-friendly website highlights her professional photo shoots, runway shows, brand collaborations and notable achievements in the fashion and modeling industry. With a strong personality, the site makes it easy to access her polaroids and social media.

The main challenge was to accommodate a large number of photos and videos while maintaining a balance between aesthetics and functionality, ensuring a clear perception.

The site features a carefully designed gallery containing the supermodel's most iconic photo shoots and runway shows.

Developing a portfolio website for a Ukrainian supermodel was an extremely rewarding experience. It not only allowed me to apply my technical skills, but also gave me the opportunity to contribute to the promotion of a talented personality in the fashion world. The site successfully showcased her outstanding career, engaging personality and unique style, further establishing her status as a leading figure in the industry.
