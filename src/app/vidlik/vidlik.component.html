Welcome to VIDLIK - your productivity partner to help you make every second count! No registration needed to use the user-friendly interface and benefit from Pomodoro's techniques. Stay focused and avoid burnout effortlessly by breaking work into intervals and short breaks. Use the Pomodoro technique by breaking up your work into focused intervals and short breaks to stay on track and maintain a balanced work-life balance.
However, to make the experience more personalized, sign up to unlock the full potential of our app by creating your own to-do lists with editable working hours, number of rounds, and break lengths and start speeding up your workflow.

To keep track of your own task list, you need to register in the app. Users can sign in in a simple way or choose Google Authentication.

After registration, all users can create and edit a personalized to-do list. Where users can change the status of the task.

If the task is in progress, it is highlighted in green and minutes on the timer are changed.
